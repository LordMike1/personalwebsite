Utilizing Brexit for Rewilding

Journalism    National News    Policy Blog    Sustainability   
Before Brexit, agriculture in the UK was subscribed to the Common Agricultural Policy (CAP) which focused on ensuring “affordable, safe and high-quality food”, supporting farmers’ incomes, and protecting the environment. This policy was used as a catch-all system, ignoring the fact that certain areas might benefit from something more bespoke. Since about 2017, DEFRA, the government department responsible for all things relating to the environment, food production, and rural affairs, has been telling farmers to prepare for a change.
